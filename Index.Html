Home

Welcome to Sta. Lucia High School, established in 1990 and legislated by Republic Act 7767 on June 19, 1994. Our school strives to provide quality education and maintain the vision and mission of the Department of Education.

With a history of excellence, SLHS has been a beacon of educational success, producing students who excel in both academic and extracurricular activities. Our dedicated teachers and staff work tirelessly to ensure every student's growth and development.

Join us in our journey to make a difference and strive for excellence. At Sta. Lucia High School, we believe in nurturing young minds to become responsible and successful individuals in society.

About Us

Sta. Lucia High School was founded through the efforts of Hon. Rufino S. Javier, the congressman of Pasig. The establishment of the school was a significant milestone for the community, providing access to quality education for the youth in the area.

The construction of the school's 24-room building began soon after the legislation, and it was formally inaugurated on March 10, 1998. Over the years, SLHS has grown in both size and reputation, continuously improving its facilities and programs.

Our school's mission is to provide a nurturing and supportive environment where students can achieve their full potential. We are committed to fostering a culture of excellence, discipline, and integrity among our students and staff.

Programs

Sta. Lucia High School offers a wide range of programs to cater to the diverse interests and needs of our students. Our Junior High School and Senior High School programs are designed to provide a strong foundation in academics and prepare students for higher education and future careers.

In addition to the standard curriculum, we offer specialized programs such as the Alternative Learning System (Non-Graded) and the Open High School Program. These programs provide flexible learning options for students who may need alternative pathways to education.

Our Senior High School offers various strands, including Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Information and Communications Technology (ICT), and Home Economics (Hotel and Restaurant Services).
